Create a complete Fasteners Business Calculator inside my website.

IMPORTANT:
- Clean industrial UI
- Fast and simple to use
- All calculations must be accurate

----------------------------------

STEP 1: RAW MATERIAL (ROD CALCULATION)

Inputs:
- Diameter (mm)
- Length (mm)
- Quantity
- Material (dropdown with factor)

Material Factors:
- MS → 0.0063
- SS → 0.0060
- Brass → 0.0073
- Aluminium → 0.0027

Formula:
Weight per piece = (D × D × L × Factor) / 1000
Total Weight = Weight per piece × Quantity

----------------------------------

STEP 2: MATERIAL COST

Input:
- Rate per kg (₹)

Formula:
Material Cost = Total Weight × Rate per kg

----------------------------------

STEP 3: LABOUR COST

Add 2 options (toggle):

1. Labour per kg
   Labour Cost = Total Weight × Rate

2. Labour per piece
   Labour Cost = Quantity × Rate

(User can enter any value manually)

----------------------------------

STEP 4: REAL COST

Formula:
Real Cost = Material Cost + Labour Cost

----------------------------------

STEP 5: PROFIT

Input:
- Profit %

Formula:
Profit Amount = Real Cost × (Profit % / 100)

----------------------------------

STEP 6: FINAL SELLING PRICE

Formula:
Final Price = Real Cost + Profit Amount

----------------------------------

STEP 7: OPTIONAL GST

Toggle ON/OFF:
If ON:
GST = Final Price × GST %
Final Price with GST = Final Price + GST

----------------------------------

OUTPUTS (IMPORTANT):

- Weight per piece (kg)
- Total Weight (kg)
- Material Cost (₹)
- Labour Cost (₹)
- Real Cost (₹)
- Profit Amount (₹)
- Final Selling Price (₹)
- Final Price with GST (if enabled)
- Rate per piece (₹)

----------------------------------

FEATURES:

- Auto calculate on input change
- Round values to 2 decimal
- Clean card layout
- Highlight Final Price
- Mobile friendly

----------------------------------

GOAL:

Create a complete calculator that helps:
- Know how much raw material is needed
- Calculate manufacturing cost
- Add profit
- Generate final selling price
